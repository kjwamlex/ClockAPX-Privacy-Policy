# Privacy Policy for ClockAPX

Effective date: March 13, 2026

ClockAPX ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information the app uses, how it is used, and your choices.

## Information We Access

### 1. App settings and customization data (stored on device)
ClockAPX stores your app preferences locally on your device, such as:
- selected clock designs and palettes
- timer and display settings
- onboarding and premium access state
- favorites and per-design customization

This data is stored in local app storage and is used only to provide app functionality.

### 2. Photos you choose
If you choose a background image using the photo picker, the selected image data is stored locally on your device to render your chosen clock background.

We do not upload your selected photos to our servers.

### 3. In-app purchases and subscriptions
ClockAPX uses Apple's StoreKit framework for purchases and subscription management. Purchase transactions are processed by Apple. We use StoreKit entitlement information to unlock premium features in the app.

### 4. Advertising (Google AdMob)
The free version of ClockAPX may display ads via Google AdMob. AdMob may collect and process data such as device identifiers, approximate location, and ad interaction data according to Google's policies.

For more details, see:
- Google Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy
- How Google uses information from sites or apps: https://policies.google.com/technologies/partner-sites

### 5. App Tracking Transparency (ATT)
On supported iOS versions, we request tracking permission before starting ad services where required. You can allow or deny tracking in the prompt, and you can change this later in iOS Settings.

## Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data.

We may share limited data with third-party service providers only as needed to operate app features:
- Apple (StoreKit/App Store purchase processing)
- Google (AdMob ad delivery and measurement)

## Data Retention

- Local app settings and selected background image data remain on your device until you delete the app or reset data.
- Purchase records are maintained by Apple according to Apple's policies.
- Ad-related data retention is governed by Google's policies.

## Your Choices

- You can deny tracking permission in the ATT prompt or later in iOS Settings.
- You can remove app data by deleting the app from your device.
- You can manage subscriptions in your Apple account subscription settings.

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be reflected by revising the effective date above.
